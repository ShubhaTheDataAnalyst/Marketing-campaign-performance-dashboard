1️⃣ Campaign Performance Overview

Which campaign generated the highest conversions

Campaigns with highest ROI

Campaigns that are underperforming

Insight Example:
“Email campaign generated the highest conversion rate compared to social media campaigns.”

2️⃣ Customer Engagement

Click-Through Rate (CTR)

Open Rate (for email campaigns)

Engagement rate across channels

Insight Example:
“Social media campaigns have high engagement but lower conversion compared to search campaigns.”

3️⃣ Conversion Analysis

Total conversions

Conversion rate by campaign

Conversion by marketing channel

Insight Example:
“Google Ads campaigns convert better than Facebook Ads for high-value customers.”

4️⃣ Cost vs Revenue

Cost per Acquisition (CPA)

Campaign spend vs revenue generated

Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)

Insight Example:
“Campaign A has the lowest CPA, making it the most cost-effective campaign.”

5️⃣ Customer Segment Insights

Performance by age group

Performance by location

Performance by device (mobile vs desktop)

Insight Example:
“Customers aged 25–34 have the highest conversion rate.”

6️⃣ Time-Based Performance

Performance by month / week

Best performing days

Seasonal trends

Insight Example:
“Campaign performance increases during weekends.”

7️⃣ Channel Performance

Compare channels:

Email

Social Media

Paid Ads

Organic Search

Insight Example:
“Organic traffic drives the highest revenue with the lowest marketing cost.”

8️⃣ Actionable Recommendations

Every dashboard should end with business recommendations.

Examples:

Increase budget for high ROI campaigns

Optimize low-performing ads

Focus on high-converting customer segments
